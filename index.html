One youtube video, one vimeo video

You're not going to see much unless you load the browser on a mobile device or reduce the desktop window browser size to under 700 pixels and refresh the page.

The best way to appreciate the benefits of this approach is to watch the network tab of your web inspector tools during a page load. When loaded at a mobile width, you should see far fewer files downloaded and a much smaller overall filesize vs a load at desktop width.

Fewer file requests means that mobile devices with less than optimal connections can avoid one of the more costly (in terms of time) operations: extra DNS lookups and upstream requests. Fewer files to download is the more obvious win.
